Rules and regulations

We are all for excitement and enthusiasm, but rules & regulations help us stay fair to all participants. If you wish to stand eligible for the grand prize or the daily prize lists, do comply with our rules & regulations, and abide by our terms & conditions.

  • Two players minimum are required to participate in the contest
  • The game must be played only on mobile phones
  • The players need to be residents and citizens of India
  • The location proximity between the participants should be 100mtrs
  • Do check the battery life of your phone in advance; make sure internet connection is at it’s best and the phone is in a good condition, to ensure that you don’t fall out of the game due to any kind of hindrance
