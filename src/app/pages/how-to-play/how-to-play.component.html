How to Play

Because simplicity and convenience if our way of life, we decided to keep the mechanics equally simple.

Step 1 – REGISTRATION

  • Sign up with your mobile number and become the admin.
  • Invite friends and family to join your group. You can invite as many people as you like to score more

STEP 2 - Start game

  • To start the game click on the “play now” button. Your group will receive an automated SMS or the group can also scan the QR code displayed on the admin screen for each group member.
  • Once your group member comes online, the admin can start the game. Minimum 2 players are required online to start the game.
  • Leave your phones on flat surface to score your points.

The game will stop:

  • If you lift the phone
  • If you toggle the game browser
  • If you answer a call
  • If your mobile runs out of battery
