How to Play

Because simplicity and convenience if our way of life, we decided to keep the mechanics equally simple.

    Step 1 – REGISTRATION
  • Add phone number and become the admin
    Step 2 – GAME CONNECTION
  • Invite friends and family to join your group. You can invite as many people as you like.
  • After the first invite to all players, each player will receive an automated SMS
  • As they acknowledge the SMS and verify themselves, they’ll be sent one last SMS to join the game.
  • Leave your phones aside and connect with each other.
