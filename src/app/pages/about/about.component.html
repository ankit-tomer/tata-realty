About Campaign / Activity

Screen space has creeped into our personal space. Hasn’t it? Mobile phones have single handedly managed to hamper quality of life and relationships, one click at a time. Don’t get us wrong, but you know we are right.

The constant scrolling, pings, updates and notifications have found a place on our dinner tables, in our bedrooms, and even bathrooms. So where’s the personal connection?

As a brand we advocate quality of life. To aid this, we struck upon a thought; to extend the concept of quality from homes to relationships as well.

So…get ready to get off your phone s and get closer. Even if it’s just for a while, let’s do it.

‘Phone Chodo Dil Jodo’ tis our effort at helping you steer away from your phones for a little time and explore the missing closeness with your loved ones. What’s more, also win exciting prizes in return.
